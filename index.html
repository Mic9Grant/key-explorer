Built on the natural (Aeolian) minor scale in minor mode. Chord symbols are generated by stacking diatonic thirds, so extensions show real key tones — e.g. the IV chord turns up maj7(♯11) and the minor v stays m7. The three keys at the bottom of the wheel are enharmonic: the ♯ Sharps / ♭ Flats toggle respells them and their relative minors — e.g. G♭ major / E♭ minor (flats) is the same as F♯ major / D♯ minor (sharps).
